NEWS

Amid massive anticipation Ferrari has launched the mighty Purosangue, a car that promises to change the landscape of performance vehicles across the globe. Powered by a V12 naturally-aspirated engine, the hallmarks of the Prancing Horse’s most powerful sports cars are present and correct, this time coupled with practicality and a raised driving height.

The Teatro del Silenzio in Lajatico, Pisa was the spectacular setting for the unveiling of the game-changing car that has Ferrari DNA at its core and innovation at its heart.


The result of a partnership between Ferrari and the Gran Turismo™ video game series, the spectacular Vision Gran Turismo is a futuristic single-seater sporting a more extreme version of the V6 engine adopted on the 296 GTB, 296 GTS and 296 GT3 and that also powers the new Ferrari 499P hypercar that will be seen at Le Mans next year.

In fact, Le Mans and the endurance races of the past were a big influence in the designing of the VGT9, with the legendary Prancing Horse prototype cars having won the famous race outright nine times between 1949 and 1965.
